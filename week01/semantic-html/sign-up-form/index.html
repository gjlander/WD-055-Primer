Our Enchanting Bootcamps

1. Hexadecimal Hexes: The Beginner's Charm

Duration: 4 weeks

Description: Dive into the basics of spellcasting—uh, we mean coding—with our introductory bootcamp. You'll learn how to cast simple loops, summon variables, and conjure up your first functions using our signature hand gestures. By the end, you'll be able to code with a mere wave of your pinky finger (or at least, you'll have a solid grasp of the fundamentals).

Magical Gesture Focus: The "Wand Wave" for initiating loops and the "Mystic Circle" for creating variables.


2. Enchantments & Encapsulation: Intermediate Wizardry

Duration: 6 weeks

Description: Ready to take your spellcasting to the next level? In this intermediate bootcamp, you'll learn the ancient secrets of object-oriented programming. Encapsulate your magic (data) within protective charms (classes) and create spells (methods) that work like magic across your entire codebase. Prepare to amaze your friends as you summon entire programs with a single gesture.

Magical Gesture Focus: The "Phantom Grasp" for object creation and the "Cloak of Shadows" for data encapsulation.


3. Dark Arts of Debugging

Duration: 5 weeks

Description: Even the most powerful wizards encounter bugs in their spells. This bootcamp teaches you the dark arts of debugging—exorcising errors from your code with the precision of a master sorcerer. You’ll learn to decipher cryptic error messages, break hexes (breakpoints), and cleanse your code of even the most sinister bugs.

Magical Gesture Focus: The "Error Vanish" flick for clearing bugs and the "Scrying Eye" to foresee potential issues.


4. Summoning the Full Stack

Duration: 8 weeks

Description: For those ready to command both front-end and back-end magic, this advanced bootcamp will turn you into a full-stack sorcerer. Master the arcane arts of HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and server-side spellcasting. By the end, you'll be able to summon entire web applications from the void, with just a few well-placed gestures.

Magical Gesture Focus: The "Elemental Weave" for front-end design and the "Shadow Portal" for back-end connectivity.
